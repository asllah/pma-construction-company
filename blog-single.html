About Us

PMA Construction Company PMA Construction Company, located at Kadampuzha, Palathingal in Malappuram district, is a trusted name in the field of home and building construction. With over three years of experience, our team of skilled professionals brings a wealth of expertise and knowledge to each project we undertake. Our company is proud to be staffed by a dedicated team of five employees, each committed to ensuring the highest standards in construction, architecture, and customer satisfaction.

At PMA Construction Company, we specialize in a wide range of services that cater to the diverse needs of our clients. From the initial stages of designing and estimation to the final touches in construction and landscape development, we offer comprehensive solutions for residential and commercial projects. We also handle crucial aspects such as building permissions, ensuring that every project complies with local regulations, and interior design, where we create spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Additionally, we provide professional survey services to ensure accuracy in measurements and site evaluations.
